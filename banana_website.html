Bananas are one of the world's most popular fruits! They're sweet, nutritious, and come in their own biodegradable packaging. Native to Southeast Asia, bananas are now grown in tropical regions around the world.

Rich in potassium and fiber, bananas are not only tasty but also great for your health. Whether you eat them raw, slice them into cereal, or blend them into a smoothie, bananas are always a-peeling!

Fun fact: the banana is technically a berry, and the banana plant is actually the world's largest herb!
